Chicken Curry

This recipe is a relatively simple and cheap one to make. You will need chicken breasts, curry sauce, onions, peppers, seasonings, and jasmine rice. It can serve around 4-6 people and can be done in around 30 minutes.

  • Vegetable oil
  • 3 chicken breasts
  • Curry sauce jar
  • Red onions
  • Assorted peppers
  • Seasonings for chicken
  • Jasmine rice
  1. Take your thawed chicken and cut them into cubes and season them.
  2. Add your vegetable oil to a pan/wok and begin cooking the chicken till it loses it's pink color.
  3. Pour your sauce jar onto the chicken and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add your onions and peppers during the simmering for desired vegetable crunch.
  5. As the chicken simmers, wash and cook 3-4 cups of jasmin rice.
  6. Create a base of rice in your bowl and throw your curry on top to enjoy.
