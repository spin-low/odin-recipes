Classic Burger

This is a classic burger, nothing special, cook it at a bbq you throw with your friends. Fun and delicious. Serves as much as you'd like

  • Hamburger buns
  • Ground beef
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Ketchup
  1. Take your thawed groundbeef, tenderize and season it, then form into patties.
  2. Bring your patties to the grill and sear both sides until cooked throughout. (OPTIONAL: ADD CHEESE TO TOP WHEN ONE SIDE IS COOKED)
  3. Take your buns and toast them slightly on the grill after burgers are cooked.
  4. Add cheese, ketchup, lettuce, and onions to your top or bottom bun.
  5. Finish by adding a nice juicy patty and enjoy!
