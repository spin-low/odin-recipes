Turkey and Salami Sandwich

This sandwich is a delicious lunch option, takes very little time, and requires no cooking unless desired

  • 2 slices of wheat bread
  • Butter
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Turkey lunch meat
  • Salami slices
  • Gouda cheese
  1. Take your wheat bread (toasted if you prefer) and spread a layer of butter on one side of one slice.
  2. Lay a slice of gouda cheese on the buttered slice.
  3. Fold 3 slices of turkey and lay them across the cheese.
  4. Lay 3-6 slices of salami on top of the turkey.
  5. Add a few onion rings and a nice layer of crunchy lettuce on top.
  6. Squish your last piece of bread on top.
  7. Cut in half and enjoy!
